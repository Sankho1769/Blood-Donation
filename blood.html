Guidelines for Donors

What you MUST do

  • Hydrate well (drink at least 500ml water).
  • Eat a healthy, iron-rich meal 3 hours prior.
  • Sleep for at least 7-8 hours the night before.
  • Wear comfortable clothing with loose sleeves.
  • Carry a valid Government Photo ID.
  • Rest for 15 mins at the center post-donation.

What you MUST AVOID

  • Avoid smoking & alcohol for 24 hours prior.
  • Do not donate on an empty stomach.
  • Avoid heavy lifting/exercise for 24 hours after.
  • Do not consume caffeinated drinks right before.
  • Do not donate if you had a tattoo in the last 6 months.
  • Do not donate if you are currently on antibiotics.
